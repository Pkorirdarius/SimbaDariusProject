We Provide For the unpreviledge communities

Pilakans Enterprise is a charity organization that was founded with the aim of providing support to underprivileged communities and promoting sustainable development. Since its inception, the organization has made significant progress in achieving its objectives and making a positive impact on the lives of people in need.



Provision basic needs to all

The primary objective of Pilakans Enterprise is to provide basic needs such as food, shelter, and education to underprivileged communities. The organization achieves this by working closely with local communities and governments to identify their specific needs and then providing appropriate support. For example, the organization has built schools in rural areas and provided scholarships to underprivileged students to help them receive a proper education.



Our achievements

One of the key achievements of Pilakans Enterprise is its ability to bring about positive change in the lives of underprivileged communities. The organization has helped to improve the standard of living of many people in need by providing them with access to basic necessities such as food, shelter, and education. This has had a direct impact on reducing poverty and improving the overall quality of life in these communities.



Promoting sustainable development

Another significant achievement of Pilakans Enterprise is its success in promoting sustainable development. The organization has helped communities to become self-sufficient by providing them with the skills and resources they need to improve their standard of living. This has resulted in improved economic conditions, as well as increased access to healthcare and education, which are crucial for sustainable development.
